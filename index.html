What We Do

Our Mission:

At Nike, our mission is to bring inspiration and innovation to every athlete in the world. Whether you're a professional athlete or just starting out, we believe that everyone has the potential to achieve greatness. That's why we are committed to creating products and experiences that help you reach your full potential, both on and off the field.

Sustainability

Our Swag:

At Nike, we believe in the power of sport to make positive changes in the world, which is why we are committed to sustainability. We are constantly striving to reduce our environmental impact by using sustainable materials, minimizing waste, and reducing our carbon footprint. We also partner with organizations that are working to make a difference in their communities, like the Nike Community Impact Fund, which supports organizations that use sport to promote social justice and equity.
